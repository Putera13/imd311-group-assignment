Return and Exchange Policy

Timeframe for Returns:

You have 30 days from the date of purchase to initiate a return.

Condition of the Shoes:

Shoes must be returned in the same condition as when purchased. This includes:

  • No significant signs of wear beyond what was described in the product listing.
  • Original shoe box and packaging (if applicable).
  • Intact original tags and labels.

Hygiene and Sanitization:

For the safety and comfort of our customers, all returned shoes must be clean and free from any odors.

Cleaning and Sanitization Process

Rest assured, our shoes undergo a thorough cleaning and sanitization process before reaching our shelves. We prioritize your well-being and the longevity of our products.
